One Software Technologies Ltd Company Profile

One Software Technologies Ltd is an Israel-based company engaged directly and through its subsidiaries in marketing and distribution of software products from Oracle, IBM and BEA. In addition, the Company distributes self-developed software and other software products developed by other manufacturers. furthermore, the Company offers supporting services, outsourcing, assimilation and implementation. Some of One Software Technologies Ltd solutions include customer relationship management software, business intelligence, project management, enterprise resource planning and integration of financials, logistical and human resources applications. Some of the Company's subsidiaries include John Bryce Technologies Ltd., Call-Up Communications and Stanga Ltd.

One Security

ONE Security is a leader in the field of information security in Israel and provides its customers with a variety of advanced and proven security solutions. Their services and products includes many and varied capabilities, which make it possible to adapt an optimal solution to the security needs of each customer. Among our services: performing penetration tests, site surveys and audits, characterization of solutions, CISO As A Service, adjusting a formal framework for the implementation of protection and control controls and more.

ONE Security employees and managers have gained extensive experience during many years of activity in senior positions in the service of the government and the business sector, in the high-tech and financial fields. Here you will find the best consultants and experts in the country, with rich knowledge and experience in the fields of application and integration, characterization, and implementation of information security and cyber solutions.

Their customer base includes leading companies in the economy and large public bodies and institutions, government ministries, cellular companies, banks, insurance companies and high-tech companies. Their customers receive a comprehensive solution that helps them deal with existing threats, and even maintain a set of controls that will be prepared to deal with future threats.
